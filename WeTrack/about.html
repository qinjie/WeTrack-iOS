Elderly folk suffering from dementia make up a significant number of missing cases reported every year in Singapore. Many of them never reunited with their family as they are often found without any identification and are unable to remember where they live. By 2030, the number of elderly with dimentia in Singapore will more than double to 103k.

This project uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) beacons to tag elderlies with dimentia. But installing this app, you are helping to locate missing elderlies through your mobile phone.

How it work

  • Dimentia elderlies are tagged with beacons and registered in system.
  • Missing elderlies are flagged in the system for tracking.
  • App on your phone will be triggered when there is missing elderly nearby.
  • App will send the beacon info and GPS location to server.
